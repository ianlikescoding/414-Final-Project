Problem

Bad or Good Grapes?

In the process of grape cultivation, fruit thinning is important for the quality and taste of the grapes. It is necessary to remove poorly formed grape berries during the early stage of fruit development. Identifying good and bad grapes helps to recognize grape quality during fruit thinning. Therefore, the goal of this project is to create a grape dataset and implement grape recognition for distinguishing between good and bad grapes.
