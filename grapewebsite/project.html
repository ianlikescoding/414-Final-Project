Project

Data

We have collected two datasets. One consists of 5000 photos of grapes taken from various angles inside a greenhouse in a vineyard during the fruit thinning period. The other dataset includes photos of good and bad grapes during maturity.

Models

We used a CNN algorithm model, with an input layer of 28x28 images and an output layer of one, where 1 represents good grapes and 0 represents bad grapes. It contains three hidden layers with a 7x7 convolution kernel(continue updata...).
